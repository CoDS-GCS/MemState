Links

Capabilities

Context without spaghetti.

Relationships tell MemState how topics depend on or relate to each other. That lets retrieval pull a small neighborhood of relevant memory instead of either guessing or dumping the whole store into a prompt.

Two ways topics connect

Extension-style links

Use when one topic naturally owns or contains the narrative of another — like a project and its milestones.

Association-style links

Use when two topics are peers that should surface together — like a customer and a contract.

How to use links well

Link sparingly and intentionally. Prefer a single strong topic with fields for small facts; promote to a linked topic when the other subject needs its own lifecycle and history.
